Paragraph Writing:
-	Main Idea (Topic sentence): states the main idea of the entire paragraph. This allows readers to understand what all the subpoints that follow are working to support.
-	Evidence: this is the information (examples, quotes, paraphrase) that are used to support and explain the claim made in the topic sentence.
-	Analysis: Analyze the evidence in your own words. Explain to readers what it means and why it is significant so they are sure to get the meaning you intend from the evidence.
-	Link: Restate the main idea of the paragraph as is presented in the topic sentence. The other option to create a paragraph transition by adding to the link the main idea that will be presented in the next paragraph with a paragraph transition.
